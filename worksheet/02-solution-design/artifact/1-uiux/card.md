# card.md - Lop giao dien

**Tinh huong xu ly**: T-03 (over-reliance) + T-01/T-02 (fairness)

## 1. Giai phap la gi?

Them mot panel "Do tin cay shortlist" trong man hinh ATS. Moi ung vien hien 3 thong tin: confidence, ly do score, va co can review tay hay khong. Neu confidence thap hoac phat hien pattern fairness-risk, UI khoa nut "Reject" va bat buoc recruiter bam "Request Review".

## 2. Vi sao sua o lop giao dien?

- Recruiter de bi anchoring vao top score, nhat la khi deadline gap.
- Rui ro xay ra o khoanh khac doc output, nen UI la lop chan cuoi.
