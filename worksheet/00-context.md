# 00-context.md - Boi canh san pham cua nhom

## 1. San pham

- **Ten san pham / bot**: CV Screen Assist
- **San pham giup ai lam gi**: Ho tro recruiter loc CV va tao shortlist vong dau cho vi tri Intern Data Analyst.
- **Nguoi dung gap san pham o dau**: ATS noi bo cua cong ty.
- **Giai doan hien tai**: Dang thu nghiem noi bo truoc khi mo rong cho toan bo team HR.

## 2. Pham vi

**AI duoc lam gi**

- Doc CV va trich xuat ky nang, kinh nghiem lien quan JD.
- Cham match score so bo theo tieu chi da cau hinh.
- Goi y ly do shortlist hoac can review.

**AI khong duoc lam gi**

- Khong duoc tu dong reject ung vien.
- Khong duoc dua ket luan cuoi cung thay recruiter.
- Khong duoc su dung thuoc tinh nhay cam (gioi tinh, tuoi, vung mien) de xep hang.

**Vi sao co gioi han nay**

Rui ro fairness trong tuyen dung cao; co rang buoc phap ly ve khong phan biet doi xu; du lieu CV thuong khong dong nhat nen can nguoi that review.

## 3. Nguoi dung

- **La ai**: Recruiter junior 1-3 nam kinh nghiem, xu ly khoi luong CV lon, can ra shortlist nhanh.
- **Ho hoi AI khi nao**: Ngay sau khi dong dot nhan CV cho vi tri internship.
- **Ho can quyet dinh gi sau khi hoi AI**: Chon danh sach phong van vong 1.
- **Khi nao ho de bi ton thuong / de hieu sai**: Khi deadline gap, de tin ranking cua AI va bo qua review tay.
- **Ho thuong tin AI den muc nao**: Thuong tin cao neu UI hien score ro rang, nhung van co thoi quen check lai mot phan.

## 4. Boi canh nganh

- **Su co tuong tu da tung xay ra**:
  - iTutorGroup (EEOC, 2023): he thong tu dong tu choi ung vien theo tuoi.
  - Vu kien Mobley v. Workday (US District Court, 2024): toa cho phep mot phan khieu kien phan biet doi xu lien quan cong cu screening AI tiep tuc.
- **Quy dinh hoac rang buoc lien quan**:
  - Nguyen tac khong phan biet doi xu trong tuyen dung.
  - NYC Local Law 144 (neu van hanh tai NYC): can bias audit cho AEDT.
- **Nguon chinh thuc nen uu tien**:
  - JD va rubric noi bo da phe duyet.
  - Chinh sach tuyen dung cua cong ty.
  - Nguon phap ly/chinh sach cong khai co uy tin.
